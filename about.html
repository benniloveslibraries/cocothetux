Coco stumbled her way into her Hooman's life as a tiny kitten in August 2015 after Hooman's boss found Coco abandoned in her neighborhood. After Hooman came home from studying abroad in Europe, Hooman's boss asked them if they were looking for a new cat. She didn't expect Hooman to say yes.

Now almost two years old, Coco has been glued to Hooman's side almost every day since her kitten days. Hooman still thinks Coco's a little kitten.

Since Coco refuses to give Hooman any privacy, Hooman has been documenting Coco's antics since June 2016 on Instagram. Coco loves the camera, and she hopes you love watching her grow!
